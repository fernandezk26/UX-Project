Sightseeing

If you love sight seeing, you will not get bored in Tanti. Most tourists spend most of their time in Taniti City, which boasts native architecture and nearby white, sandy beaches that encircle Yellow Leaf Bay.

Book a city tour to see some of the unique architecture in Taniti.
