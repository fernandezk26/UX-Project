Outdoor Recreation

If you are looking for some wonderful natural and outdoor experiences, Taniti has got you covered! From volcano, to mountain, to sea, there is bound to be something fun for everyone. Check out some of our most popular outdoor activities below.

Taniti has am active volcano that can be hiked. Make it to the top for some spectacular views!

Want to see the ocean from a different view? Book a snorkeling trip to see some of the beautiful marine life.

Taniti is full of Rainforest's. There are some great hikes and nature walks through the forests. Book a hiking guide today!

The best way to see a large part of the island quickly is by taking the zipline ride! Great for adrenaline junkies.

Taniti is a large fishing port. You can book a fishing trip to experience a day in the life of one of the many local fisherman.
