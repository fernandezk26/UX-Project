Attractions and venues

There are many things to do in the island aside from outdoors and sightseeing. Taniti also has several museums, breweries and club, and other attractions you can visit.

Taniti has its own local microbrewery. They have a wide variety of beers made house.

Taniti has a new dance club in town! DJ's play till sun up on weekends. Go here to dance the night away and have a night to remember.

Taniti has a few different arcades. All are guaranteed to be fun for the whole family.

(Coming Soon) Taniti is in the process of creating a new 9 hole golf course. This will be a one of the kind golf course set in a beautiful island landscape.

Visit one of Taniti's bowling alleys for a night of fun.

Taniti has several art galleries. Visit one to see some of the islands talented local artists in action.

In the mood for a movie? Don't worry Taniti has a few theaters that have you covered.
